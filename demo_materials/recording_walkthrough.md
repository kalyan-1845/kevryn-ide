# 🎬 Faculty Masterclass: Step-by-Step Recording Script
**Setup Phase (Friend/Operator)** | **Narration (You/Speaker)**

---

## 🏛️ Phase 1: Institutional Management
| Step | Action (Friend) | Script (You) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | Click **'My Courses'** in the sidebar. | "Sir, every successful lab starts here. We’ve developed a Smart Roster system where instructors can manage courses and thousands of students with ease." |
| 2 | Click on a Course (e.g., CSE-B) to show students. | "Observe the granularity. We can track enrollment, attendance, and student profiles directly within the IDE." |

---

## 📝 Phase 2: Academic Preparation
| Step | Action (Friend) | Script (You) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 3 | Click **'Assignments'** -> Click **'Create New'**. | "Next, we prepare the content. The 'Assignments' module allows us to create specific coding challenges, set constraints, and schedule deadlines." |
| 4 | Click **'Aptitude Tests'** -> Show a sample test. | "For a 360-degree evaluation, we’ve developed an Aptitude Engine. This is perfect for placement training, offering randomized MCQs that are auto-graded by the system." |

---

## 🎯 Phase 3: Live Lab Deployment (THE CORE)
| Step | Action (Friend) | Script (You) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 5 | Click **'Live Monitor'** -> Select Course -> **'Start Session'**. | "Now, the Live Lab begins. With a single click, every student’s IDE becomes a monitored workstation. The system establishes a 100ms synchronization link." |
| 6 | Point to the **Student Roster** in the Monitor. | "Look at this cockpit. I see exactly who is Active, who is Idle, and who is falling behind. No more walking around to check screens." |
| 7 | **CRITICAL**: Click a student's name to show their **Live Code**. | "We can dive into any student's workspace at any second. I see their logic flow, their errors, and their progress in real-time. This is total transparency." |

---

## 🦁 Phase 4: 'Beast' Monitoring & Accountability
| Step | Action (Friend) | Script (You) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 8 | Hover over the red **'Tab Switch'** or **'Paste'** alert icons. | "And here is the 'Beast' engine. If a student tries to cheat by switching tabs or pasting code, the system flags them instantly. High-stakes exams are now 100% secure." |
| 9 | Click **'Gradebook'** to show cumulative scores. | "Everything flows back into the Gradebook. It’s automated, objective, and eliminates manual paperwork for our faculty." |

---

## 📊 Phase 5: Conclusion & Reporting
| Step | Action (Friend) | Script (You) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 10 | Click **'Reports'** -> **'Generate PDF'**. | "We conclude with data. Every lab session generates a forensic report—perfect for NAAC documentation and academic reviews. Kevryn is ready for ACE Engineering College." |

---

### 💡 Tips for a GREAT Recording:
- **Pacing**: Tell your friend to wait 2-3 seconds after each click before moving to the next step so the viewer can see the UI clearly.
- **Enthusiasm**: Use a powerful, confident voice when talking about the "Live Monitor" and "Beast Engine"—this is what will impress the Chairman most!
